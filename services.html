Expert Deck Solutions Tailored to Your Alaskan Lifestyle

Custom designs and quality craftsmanship for decks that withstand Alaska's unique challenges

At Deck Masters, we offer a comprehensive range of deck services designed to bring your outdoor vision to life and withstand the unique challenges of the Alaskan environment. From initial design to final construction and even urgent repairs, our skilled team is equipped to handle projects of any complexity with precision and care.

Our Core Services

Custom Deck Design & Consultation

Have a vision? We'll help you refine it. Our experts work with you to design a deck that perfectly suits your needs, aesthetic preferences, and property. We consider everything from materials like cedar, Trex, and glass to features like solariums, pergolas, and wheelchair accessibility.

New Deck Construction

Building a brand new deck? We specialize in high-quality construction, ensuring a sturdy, beautiful, and long-lasting addition to your home. Our team is experienced in building on challenging terrains, including hillsides, and in all Alaskan seasons, even winter.

Deck Replacement & Demolition

Is your old deck damaged, deteriorating, or simply not meeting your needs? We handle complete tear-downs and replacements, transforming your outdoor space efficiently. We take care of removing your "crumbling" or "rickety" old deck and building a solid new foundation for outdoor enjoyment.

Deck Repair & Restoration

Facing issues like damaged railings, stairs, rot, or even sudden ice damage? We provide prompt and reliable repair services to restore the safety and beauty of your deck. We've saved the day for clients needing urgent repairs, even right before a house sale!

Complex Project Management

Don't let project complexity overwhelm you. We manage all aspects, including navigating municipal permitting, working around tight utility corridors, installing pilings, and incorporating features like hot tubs.

Additions & Enhancements

Looking to expand your current deck or add new features? We can build multi-level decks, add pergolas with privacy walls, decorative lattice trim, "dog-proof" fencing, and more to enhance your outdoor living space.
